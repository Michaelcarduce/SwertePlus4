About Monopoly Live

Monopoly Live is a specialty online version of Monopoly, a board game focused around a large vertically designed wheel. The main objective of the game is simple: The actual performance is wherein a live host spins the wheel while the players have to place their bets on the segment where the wheel shall stop. This format is actually inspired partly by Dream Catcher, which is a well-known live game; however, the twists incorporated here are Monopoly-based and thus results in a different game from Dream Catcher.

There are numbers 1, 2, 5 and 10 that the players can stake and there are the special lines 2 ROLLS and 4 ROLLS which trigger the bonuses. There is yet another opportunity which is known as ‘CHANCE’ segments within the wheel in which the contestant can choose to accept cash rewards or multipliers. The virtual MR. MONOPOLY is featured in the game and they get anxious throughout waiting for whether they will form the winning segment or not.

How to Play Monopoly Live

Monopoly Live is very friendly and would not require extensive learning especially from a first-time player. CHANCE Segments:

Every time when the wheel stops on the “CHANCE” sector MR. MONOPOLY hands out a Chance card which reads either, “Collect a Cash Reward”, which grants a random amount of money, or a “2, 3, 4, 5 or 10 Times” which gives a multiplier bonus. As we have seen, in case of a multiplier bonus, all bets are to be kept spinning and the wheel is spun again.
