What is Baccarat?

Baccarat is a sort of card game that has its application in several casinos and the primary aim of the game is to get close to number nine. Played between the player and the banker (dealer), each card has a specific value:

Aces: 1

2-9: Face value

10s and face cards: 0

The cards are arranged and exposed and the house along with the hand that totals to 9 is given the win. There are three types of bets that can be made: player, banker, and tie.
